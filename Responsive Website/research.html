Research Interests:

In my research I employ techniques from geometric analysis to solve problems in geometry. I'm primarily interested in generalizations of symplectic geometry, such as b-symplectic (log-symplectic) structures. My thesis advisor is Pierre Albin.

I also consider various topics in analytic number theory.

Past Projects:

Stochastic topology1 with Stacey Butler and Argen West under the supervision of Yuliy Baryshnikov. A PDF report of our work can be found here. (Summer 2013)

Cluster algebras and integrable systems1 under the supervision of Maarten Bergvelt and Rinat Kedem. A report of my work can be found here. (Summer 2012)
